About Us

The Korean American Student Association (KASA) is a student organization at the University of San Francisco that aims to promote Korean culture and heritage. We strive to create a welcoming community for all students, regardless of their background, and to provide a space for students to learn more about Korean culture and history. Our organization hosts a variety of events throughout the year, including cultural performances, food tastings, and social gatherings. We also work to raise awareness about social issues affecting the Korean American community and to support Korean American students on campus. We welcome students of all backgrounds to join us and learn more about Korean culture and heritage.
